About: HarLeigh Haynie,BS,MB is a biostatistician and emerging researcher with interests in statistical methodology, data science, and healthcare applications. Her work focuses on developing rigorous and reproducible analytical approaches for understanding complex health data and supporting evidence-based decision-making in clinical and public health settings. She is currently building expertise in research design, statistical computing, and methodological development in preparation for doctoral training in Applied Science and Technology. Research Interests: Her research interests lie at the intersection of statistical methodology and healthcare data science, with a focus on both theoretical development and applied impact. She is particularly interested in advancing methods that improve the analysis, interpretation, and reproducibility of complex biomedical data. Key areas of interest include: - Development of statistical methods for complex and high-dimensional health data - Reproducible research practices and computational workflows in biostatistics - Application of data science techniques to healthcare and clinical research problems - Methodological innovation to improve evidence-based decision-making in medicine - Integration of statistical modeling with real-world health data systems
